Bakery Competitive Shopping Tool

Walmart Bakery Seasonal Buying Team

📌 Purpose

This tool helps vendors capture publicly available competitive retail pricing and merchandising data from store visits. Data collected supports the Walmart Bakery Seasonal Buying team in assortment planning and pricing strategy.

✅ What TO Capture

  • Retail shelf prices (visible to any shopper)
  • Promoted / sale prices & signage
  • Product photos from the shelf
  • Pack size, oz, item descriptions
  • Fixture / mod layout photos
  • Store name, city, state

⛔ What NOT to Capture

  • Cost data, invoices, or margin info
  • Vendor agreements or contract terms
  • Internal competitor documents
  • Employee names or personal info
  • Any non-public or proprietary data

🔒 Confidentiality

This tool is provided by the Walmart Bakery Seasonal Buying Team for use exclusively in support of Walmart bakery merchandising efforts. Do not share this tool, its URL, or data collected with anyone outside of your working relationship with Walmart Bakery. All data submitted is for internal Walmart use only.
